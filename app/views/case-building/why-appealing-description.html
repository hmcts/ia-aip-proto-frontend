Tell us why you're appealing

Before you start

It will help if you:

  • Creafully read your Home Office decision letter
  • Try to understand the parts of your claim the Home Office disagrees with
  • Respond to some or all the things the Home Office disagrees with
  • Include evidence where you can

You might also want to:

  • Tell us about anything new that will affect your case since you made your appeal
  • Include evidence where you can

If you need help understanding the Home Office decision letter or with any other part of your appeal you should:

  • Try to find legal advice
  • Find organisations that can support you
  • Call the Tribunal on 0300 123 1711 oe email customer.service@justice.gov.uk
