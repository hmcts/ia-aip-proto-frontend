What happens next

  • A case worker will review your answer. You may be asked to answer more questions if the case worker needs more information
  • When the case worker has all the information they need, they will ask the Home Office to review your case
  • The Home Office will either withdraw or the case will go to a hearing

If you have any questions, call the Tribunal on 0300 123 1711 or email customer.service@justice.gov.uk
