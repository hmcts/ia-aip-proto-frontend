What happens next

A case worker will look at your hearing needs. If you have asked for an interpreter, step-free access or a hearing loop, these will be provided. If you have asked for anything else , it will be considered but not always provided.

You will be sent a Notice of Hearing soon. This will include details of what will be provided at the hearing.
